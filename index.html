About Me

Hi there! I’m Daniele Bifolco, a Ph.D. student in Information Technologies for Engineering at the University of Sannio, Benevento, Italy. My journey in tech began at the University of Salerno, where I earned my Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Engineering in 2020 and later my Master’s in Computer Science in 2023. I’ve been on an exciting path of discovery ever since, diving into the fascinating intersections of software, artificial intelligence, and everything in between.

But there’s more to me than just research papers and code. I’m a passionate music enthusiast who believes every good line of code deserves a killer playlist. (Pro tip: classical for debugging, rock for breakthroughs!) Oh, and if it’s shiny and has an Apple logo on it, I probably already have it—or it’s on my wishlist. Consider me your local Apple aficionado.

I also have a big love for traveling. Discovering new places, connecting with diverse cultures, and seeing the world through fresh perspectives are what keep my curiosity alive and my creativity flowing. Whether it’s strolling through historic cities or trying out local cuisines, I believe every journey adds a new melody to life’s playlist.

When I’m not geeking out over AI or software engineering, you’ll find me experimenting with tech gadgets, exploring new music, or trying to convince people that yes, Macs are better.
